A 2000 years history!

 The Lyon city didn't made itself in one day, named "LUGDUNUM" by Roman's Emperors the legend says that Juluis Ceasar climbed the Fouviere mount, just above the actual city of Lyon and said: "This is a great place to build the main city of Gaul". This was 2000 years ago and was apparently right! The Roman amphitheater stayed there trough the ages and welcome nowadays no more old Roman artists, but singers, music groups, and music festivals.

The "Confluence" Area

 Welcome here to the new Lyon! -Confluence Area- is now the "greenest" area of the downtown, the building established there over the 10 last years produce as mutch energy as the inhabitants need to leave inside. This project of renovation of the Lyon old downtown area is the biggest among all Europeens countries. The establishment of "zero" pollution buildings wasn't that easy at the begining, the Old downtown use to be one of the unsafest place of the city, placed under the reign organised crime.
